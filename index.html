About SRKR

WELCOME SRKREC!

Welcome to SRKREC! Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKREC), established in 1980, is a self-financed academic institution of coeducation striving to provide a high quality technical education to engineering aspirants. Being one of the premier and well-established technical institutions of the country, it continues to render service to the nation and the world at large with its alumni holding highly prestigious positions and making substantial contribution. The president of the institution Sri. S. Prasada Raju carries forward the vision of his father Late Sri Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju , a philanthropist, driving the institution on the academic path of excellence. The college is replete with lush coconut trees spanning across a plot area of 30 acres at China Amiram, Bhimavaram, on Bhimavaram-Vijayawada state highway and it is also well-connected to all other places by road and rail transport.

SRKR Principle

I welcome you to the online home of SRKR Engineering College, China Amiram and thank you for your interest in our institution. SRKR Engineering College is the cradle of technical education in the Godavari Delta and has been a launch pad for many dreams to become global citizens for the past forty years. The first batch students of this college, now in the twilight years of their careers have been guiding stars and luminaries in their organisations and their communities, globally. Our alumni consistently acknowledge the significance of their college life in shaping their careers and family life
